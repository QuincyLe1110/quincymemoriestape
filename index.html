My High School Memories Tape

I don't know about you but high school is the best years of my life.

Even though it's not a place in which I thrived and grew up, doesn't mean it's not worth remembering. This project is for rewinding myself those good old days with my high school fellows.

My High School History

Ashfield College - Dublin, Ireland

Octorber, 2019 - March, 2020

Ashfield College is the destination that I chose for my final high school year because I wanted a better education. Although I only spent 6 months here, I have accessed to new knowledge and grown up a lot. I have made a lot of new friends of different origins, through which I could explore and understand more about their cultures and religions.

However, the important lesson I learned during this time of staying is never taking things for granted. Due to the Co-vid 19, my high school years ended suddenly on a random day in March while I was so delighted as I didn't have to sit my Mock exams. Just when I received news that the school would close until September did I truly realize that philosophy. Not having a graduation, yearbook photo are the biggest loss in every student's life.

Phan Chau Trinh - Da Nang, Vietnam

September, 2017 - May, 2019

Phan Chau Trinh High School is probably the place contains my youth and all of my high school memories. At this place, I actually felt like a wild and free teenager who is full of energies all the time and lived like there's no tomorrow. What can I say, it is the best times of me with my peers, carefree and enjoy every moments of life.

School Activities

All my memories probably bound with school activities as in those times, our class truly become a community, united and worked together to get jobs done.

Gallery

Every year our school holds a talent competition between classes called "Shine". This is the first Shine competition I took part in with my classmates. Eventhough we didn't make it to next round, we had a lot fun and got to know more about each other

At 11th Grade, I continue to participate in Shine competition. Sadly, this was my last time to stand on the Shine stage. It was really a marvellous and emotional performance that I will never forget, especially the moment when we got the ticket to the next round.

Everytime someone mentions camping, I just want to burst out in tears. No cap, camping is the most beautiful time in any Phan Chau Trinh High School Students' memories. It taught us the discipline, the creativity, the ability to work with each other to complete tasks. It created a foundation for a united class. It glued us together in an unexpected way. Although there are fights, arguements, disaprovements, stresses and times we don't even talk to each other and want to quit it all, we have made it through. Later, when we sat back with each other, we all agree the fact that this is the best thing that ever happened to us and wished we could go back to that times.
